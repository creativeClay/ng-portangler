Marie Dubois is a virtuoso violinist whose work is deeply rooted in the musical traditions of Brittany. With over 15 years of professional experience, she has captivated audiences across Europe with her soulful performances and technical brilliance. Marie began her journey with the violin at the age of six, studying at the Conservatoire de Rennes. Her passion for Breton folk music led her to develop a unique style that blends classical precision with the raw, emotional energy of traditional Celtic tunes. She has released three critically acclaimed albums and is a sought-after collaborator for folk and classical projects alike.

Her performances are known for their emotional depth and technical precision, seamlessly weaving together the ancient melodies of her homeland with contemporary classical techniques. Marie continues to tour internationally while also dedicating time to teaching young musicians the art of traditional Breton music.
